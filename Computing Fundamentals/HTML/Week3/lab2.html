Carrot Cake Recipe

Enjoy this recipe!


Ingredients

  1. 2 cup (260 grams) all-purpose flour
  2. 2 teaspoons baking soda
  3. 1/2 teaspoon fine sea salt
  4. 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnarmon
  5. 1 1/4 cups (295 ml) canola or other vegetable oil
  6. 1 cup(200 grams) granulated sugar
  7. 1 cup (190 ml) lightly packed brown sugar
  8. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  9. 4 large eggs, at room temperature
  10. 3 cups (300 grams) granted peeled carrots, 5 to 6 medium carrots
  11. 1 cup (100 grams) coarsely chopped pecans
  12. 1/2 cup (65 grams) raisins
